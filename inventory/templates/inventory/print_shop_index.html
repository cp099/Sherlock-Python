Printing

Open the following page and follow the given steps to get your prints:

  • Go to the print dialog by pressing Command+P (Mac) or Control+P (Windows/Linux), or by using the browser's toolbar.
  • Adjust the zoom until the width of the labels are correct.
  • Proceed to printing.
Open Print Page

Why are we relying on the browser to generate print files?

This is due to the fact that a. most browsers have reliable printing systems and b. this removes quite a lot of complexity from the codebase. When a feature is present, why not use it?
